VIDEO  [CMP] PROMOTIONAL [TRN] AUDIOVISUAL TEATRO REAL




UN MISMO COMPÁS.

FINAL INTERNSHIP PROJECT.




IT IS A PROJECT COMMISSED BY THE AUDIOVISUAL DEPARTMENT OF THE TEATRO REAL DE MADRID, AS THE FINAL WORK OF MY INTERNSHIP, WHERE I WAS ASKED TO CREATE AN AUDIOVISUAL PIECE THAT WOULD DOCUMENT EVERYTHING THAT HAPPENS BEYOND THE STAGE.

THE PROPOSAL IS A FILM THAT SHOWS AN INTIMATE JOURNEY THROUGH THE DEPARTMENTS THAT CONTRIBUTE TO THE FUNCTIONING OF A PRODUCTION, AS AN ODE TO ALL THE TECHNICIANS WHO MOVE AROUND BACKSTAGE, AWAY FROM THE ARTISTS, AND WHOSE WORK IS OFTEN OVERLOOKED OR LITTLE RECOGNIZED.

THE PROJECT MAKES A PARODY OF ALL THAT THIS WORK IS THOUGHT TO BE, CONTRASTING IT WITH WHAT IT ACTUALLY ENTAILS.

THE INTENTION IS NOT ONLY TO RECOGNIZE THE EFFORT AND RESPONSIBILITY OF THE WORKERS, BUT ALSO TO PROMOTE THE GENERAL PUBLIC'S AWARENESS OF ALL THAT MUST WORK FROM WITHIN IN ORDER TO ENJOY THE FINAL RESULT.

THIS PROJECT INVOLVES ON MY SIDE A PREVIOUS RESEARCH, A MIMICRY WITH THE DEPARTMENT ITSELF, A PRE-PRODUCTION PROCESS AND THE SUBSEQUENT FILMING AND EDITING STAGE. ALSO POST-PRODUCTION AND COLOR, AS WELL AS THE EDITING AND MODIFICATION OF THE CHOSEN MUSIC.

SINDROME KANE - RESPIRAR

VIDEOCLIP FOR THE SPANISH ROCK BAND.




'RESPIRAR' TALKS ABOUT THE FEELING OF LOOKING FOR THE BEAUTY OF THINGS WHEN LIFE TENDS TO GET COMPLICATED ON ITS OWN. TO STEP OUT OF THE WAY AND LOOK OUTSIDE, TOWARDS WHAT IS NOT VISIBLE TO THE NAKED EYE.

A TRIBUTE TO THE HUMANE IN A COMPILATION OF VIDEOS STOLEN ON THE STREET FROM PEOPLE WHO SHARE THIS ATTITUDE TOWARDS LIFE.

VIDEO  [COM] MUSIC VIDEO [CLB] MUSIC KANAI




KANAI - BURN MY WAY

VIDEOCLIP FOR THE SPANISH INDIE BAND.




BURN MY WAY IS ONE OF THE SONGS OF THE EXPERIMENTAL MUSIC PROJECT KANAI. IT TALKS ABOUT A STRANGE INNER FEELING, OF NOT BELONGING TO ANY SPECIFIC PLACE BUT ALSO NOT KNOWING HOW TO CHANGE.

THIS WEIRDNESS IS REPRESENTED THROUGH MOVEMENT, BOTH OF THE BODY AND THE CAMERA, AND IS COMPLEMENTED BY THE CHOICE OF THE LENS.

THIS PROJECT IS STARRED BY ME, AS A DANCER, AND LATER ALSO EDITED BY ME. THE RECORDING IS PAULA SÁNCHEZ'S.

VIDEO  [PSN] SHORT FILM [COL] SOCIAL FEELMOTION




THE INSIDE OF THE OUTSIDE

NOMINATED FOR BEST SHORT FILM IN THE SOCIAL ESSAY CATEGORY AT THE FEELMOTION INTERNATIONAL FESTIVAL.




WITH THIS PROJECT I TRY TO TELL THE STORY OF AN ADDICTION, BUT NOT FROM THE ADDICT'S POINT OF VIEW, BUT FROM HIS CLOSEST ENVIRONMENT. AN INSIDE VIEW OF THE OUTSIDE OF THE DISEASE.

IT TALKS ABOUT THE SLOW DETERIORATION, ABOUT HOW LITTLE BY LITTLE EVERYTHING ROTS WHEN THE ADDICTION BECOMES THE ONLY IMPORTANT THING, WHEN NOTHING ELSE IS RELEVANT ANYMORE. WHEN THEY TURN AWAY FROM EVERYTHING.

IT IS OFTEN TOLD HOW HARD IT IS TO GO THROUGH THIS, BUT IT IS NOT TALKED ABOUT HOW HEARTBREAKING IT IS FOR THEIR LOVED ONES. THIS IS THEIR STORY.
